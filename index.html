Production Approach

THREE PHASES. ONE STORY.

1
Pre-Launch · Half Day Shoot

THE WARM-UP

We arrive before the crowds to capture the space in its purest form — atmosphere, architecture, branded cues, and teaser-worthy movement moments. The goal is to create just enough intrigue to build anticipation without revealing everything.


Focus: Vertical teasers optimised for Instagram Stories & Reels pre-seeding.

Minimum Output

10–15 vertical video clips
1 teaser edit (10–15 sec)
Delivery within 24h post-shoot
2
Launch Day · Full Shoot Day

THE MOMENT

Our full crew deploys across the park — one lead cinematographer on hero moments, a second camera operator capturing reactions and crowd energy, and a dedicated interview setup for VIP and creator talent. We work in sync with your event flow, not against it.


Focus: Hero footage, 4 structured interviews, and 20–30 select clips for reels and ads.

Minimum Output

Full shoot day (8–10 hrs)
4 interviews captured
20–30 select highlight clips
3
Post-Launch · Editing & Delivery

THE AFTERMATH

Post-production is where we turn raw footage into a reusable content engine. Our editors work from a pre-approved mood reference, syncing to brief on energy, pacing, and brand tone. Every deliverable goes through an internal review before reaching the client.


Focus: Hero recap, 8 platform-ready reels, curated b-roll library, and clean delivery.

Minimum Output

1 hero recap (60–90s, 2 formats)
8 vertical reels (15–30 sec)
60-clip b-roll pack + log sheet
